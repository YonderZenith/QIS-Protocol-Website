Privacy Statement

QIS Protocol processes no personal data at the protocol layer. This site uses standard aggregate analytics and respects GDPR, CCPA, and UK GDPR frameworks.

Effective: 2026-04-13 · Last modified: 2026-04-13 · Yonder Zenith LLC · Phoenix, Arizona

Three-sentence summary

1. Protocol layer: QIS Protocol processes only protocol-level routing metadata — no personal data, health records, or identifiable information is collected, transmitted, or stored by the QIS routing layer.

2. Site analytics: Analytics on this site are limited to aggregate, anonymized visitor behavior collected via Google Analytics 4 and company-level identification via Leadfeeder; no data is sold or shared with third parties.

1. Data controller

The data controller for qisprotocol.com is Yonder Zenith LLC, Phoenix, Arizona, USA. The contact person for all data protection matters is Christopher Thomas Trevethan, Founder — ctt0330@gmail.com.

2. What the QIS Protocol does NOT collect

The QIS (Quadratic Intelligence Swarm) protocol itself — independent of this website — is designed to be privacy-by-architecture. The following is NEVER collected, transmitted, or stored by the protocol:

  • Personal data (names, emails, identifiers)
  • Health records, clinical data, or Protected Health Information (PHI)
  • Raw datasets of any kind
  • Patient-identifiable information
  • Biometric data, genetic data, or other special categories (Article 9 GDPR)
  • Authentication credentials or session tokens

QIS routes only ~512-byte outcome packets containing a semantic fingerprint, outcome label, confidence score, and integrity checksum. Raw data never leaves the source node by architectural design.
